# STAR Story

## Situation
Enterprise dashboards and AI agents can disagree when teams define the same KPI differently.

## Task
Build a governed semantic metrics trust layer that makes metric definitions consistent, explainable, versioned, and auditable.

## Action
Created synthetic enterprise data, YAML metric definitions, DuckDB warehouse, contract validation, metric calculation, conflict detection, lineage, trust scoring, AI readiness, approval workflow, API, dashboard, tests, CI, and Docker.

## Result
Delivered a reproducible local platform that demonstrates semantic governance and safe metric consumption for BI and AI systems.
